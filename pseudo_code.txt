- Verify that the modulus_size is between 2048 and 4096
- Verify the public exponent (e) such as e is odd and between 2^16 and 2^256


Initiate:

Algorithm:
1) put modulus_size in RSA_KEYPAIR
2) put public exponent in RSA_KEYPAIR

Return:
1) If success: RETURN_SUCCES (=1)
